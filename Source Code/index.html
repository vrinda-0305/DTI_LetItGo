LET IT GO is a unique mental health website. It acts as a social platform for people to connect, share experiences, and offer support to one another, but it does so anonymously, allowing people who share similar feelings to remain together and feel like they are part of everyone rather than isolated. People who share similar mental health experiences can benefit from this kind of website because it can help alleviate feelings of isolation and loneliness. Users can connect with others who understand what they are going through and participate in discussions, giving them the impression that someone is always available to them. Mentors and volunteers will be divided up in the project, and they will be able to spot "alarming phrases" in communication and point people to mentors when needed. The objective is to provide individuals with a secure and encouraging environment in which they can connect and forge meaningful relationships with others who comprehend their challenges.
